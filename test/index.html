Plotly.js: 3D Volatility Surface

Quantitative Modeling

Visualizing an Options Implied Volatility Surface. Strike prices are mapped against Time-to-Expiry to model the volatility smile curve in three dimensions.

  • Interactive Rotations: Drag your mouse over the 3D surface plot to rotate, pan, or zoom in real-time.
  • Asymmetry & Smile: Shows higher implied volatility for out-of-the-money (OTM) puts, characteristic of equity options.
  • Term Structure: The volatility curvature flattens out as expiry time ($t$) increases.

D3.js: Interactive Entity Graph

MarTech Knowledge Graph

A force-directed network diagram showing nodes (entities) and links (ontological connections) within a system.

  • Draggable Nodes: Click and drag any node to test the elastic gravity simulation in D3.
  • Interactive Zoom: Double-click to zoom in, or scroll your mouse wheel to zoom/pan.
  • Ontology mapping: Demonstrates node links between platforms, databases, and metrics.

MathJax: Equation Typesetting

Render mathematical formulations dynamically using LaTeX syntax. Enables clean, scalable mathematics across all screens.

Black-Scholes Option Pricing PDE

Describes the pricing of derivative financial contracts over time.

$$\frac{\partial V}{\partial t} + \frac{1}{2}\sigma^2 S^2 \frac{\partial^2 V}{\partial S^2} + r S \frac{\partial V}{\partial S} - r V = 0$$

Kelly Sizing Allocation

Optimal allocation of investment capital to maximize growth rate under risk.

$$f_i^* = p_i - \frac{1 - p_i}{\text{ROAS}_i}$$
